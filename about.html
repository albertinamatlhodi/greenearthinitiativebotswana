Our Story in Botswana

From grassroots beginnings to nationwide impact

Our History

Founded in 2023 by Albertina Matlhodi, Green Earth Initiative Botswana began as a small tree-planting project in Gaborone. Today, we operate across all 10 districts of Botswana with over 50 full-time staff and thousands of volunteers.
