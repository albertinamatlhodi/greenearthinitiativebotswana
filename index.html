Our Mission in Botswana

Green Earth Initiative Botswana is dedicated to protect Botswana's diverse wildlife, combat desertification, and preserve our natural resources for future generations.

Featured Projects in Botswana

Kalahari Tree Planting Initiative

Combating desertification by planting drought-resistant native species across the Kalahari region, with over 15,000 trees planted since 2023.

Okavango Delta Conservation
